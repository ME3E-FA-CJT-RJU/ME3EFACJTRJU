The Marian Missionaries of the Holy Cross is a religious congregation of priests and brothers founded in the Philippines dedicated to serve the youth, especially those in the public schools, and those in prison through moral values formation and renewal.

Missionary of the Holy Cross aims to help the students who have lost their way on there path to rightiousness where they are blinded by the material things to help them "fit in". As the current generation rely mostly on technology, they have forgotten the Word of God and to spread His love to people. The students of Technological University of the Philippines - Visayas are one of the pretigious school that our organization wanted to reach out to as we aim to help those students who have lost their way into the path of Christ. One can conquer his/her fear by the absence of arrogance and incompetency. Let us start the change that we want to see, the change that begins in me.
