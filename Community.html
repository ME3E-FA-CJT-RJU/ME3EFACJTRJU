When the pandemic ensued in 2020, our brothers and priests were able to reach out to our youth members through various online ministries. Our usual youth activities, primarily, the regular Spiritual Readings (SRs) were held through different social media platforms. When lockdowns were lifted, members were visited individually at home while observing strict health protocols. And when restrictions on public gatherings gradually eased, some core groups were met in churches or barrio chapels to give spiritual inputs and relief goods, especially, those on Negros Island. This year, our campus youth apostolate gradually resumes in:

  • 58 high schools in Metro Manila & Rizal | YMC

  • 69 high schools in Negros Occidental | YMC

  • 44 high schools in Bataan Province | DYMC

  • 32 high schools in Dumaguete Province | DYMC

On the other hand, 6 parishes entrusted to our MMHC priests have also resumed their formation program for their parish youth groups. These parishes are:

  • Divine Mercy Parish, Dumaguete;

  • Immaculate Conception Parish, Bataan;

  • Holy Family Parish, Artesia, USA;

  • St. John of God Parish, Norwalk, USA | with YMC Chapter;

  • Shrine of our Lady of China, Chiayi, Taiwan;

  • Holy Family Parish, Hsin Chu, Taiwan

We thank our General Pastoral Director, Fr. Eymard Gayotin, MMHC, and Director for Mission, Fr. William Padios, MMHC, for their guidance. Over the years, some of our priests were appointed youth directors in some Dioceses (Balanga, Lingayen-Dagupan, Hsin Chu in Taiwan, Chiayi in Taiwan) and we praise God for these opportunities to serve the youth on a larger scale.

Mabuhay si Kristo! Mabuhay si Maria! Mabuhay ang mga Kabataan!
